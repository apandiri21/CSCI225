To use this calculator, press any number you see on the screen as you see fit. Once you have selected your first number(s), pick your operator sign (Plus / +, Minus / -, Multiply / *, or Divide / /), select your next number(s), and finally hit the equals (=) button to see the end result.

If you have made a mistake with your equation at any point, just click the "C" button. This will clear your equation and allow you to input it from the very beginning.

Decimals are supported by this program! To use decimals, click the "." button.

If you are still unsure as to how the program works, please look at the accompanying screenshots and videos that will walk you through the process step-by-step.
